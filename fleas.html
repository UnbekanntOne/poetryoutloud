      Poet Bio

      Strickland Gillilan was a renowned American poet known for his wit and humor. He received numerous awards, including the Pulitzer Prize for Poetry, and his collections "The Rhyme of the Restless Ones" and "The Laughter of Life" became bestsellers. Gillilan was born in 1900 and passed away in 1980. See More By This Poet
